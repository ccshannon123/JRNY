WHAT WE DO

It's no secret locals know best. It's also not surprising that travelers are spending more time planning for vacations than ever before. That's why we are introducing Jrny, your first stop when planning your next vacation. We are connecting travelers with locals who provide the most effective solution to travel planning. Whether you want to be fully involved in the planning process or not at all, an off-the-beaten-path experience or full-on {the classic} tourist experience, or somewhere in between, our locals are here to show you why they love the place they call home. .

All locals are vetted to ensure you are receiving the best possible experience. They may be bloggers, avid travelers, or just active locals who love their town. Each local specializes in a few areas they know best (ie. Live music, shopping, food) to ensure you are receiving the best advice that you want to know about. But remember, even if they don't specialize in what you are looking for, I bet they know who to ask!

With each Jrny destination you are provided with the following:

  • Pre-trip Consultation
  • Personalized Itinerary
  • Local Survival Guide
